INDES broadcaster

How to use

Welcome to Indes broadcaster, a project developer for the course INDES (Interfaces e Design). This page serves as a quick tutorial on how to use the app.

* The top cameras are your webcams - click them to broadcast their content * On the left side is the youtube Area * copy Youtube ID's onto the textbox to store them in the list * reorder the list and play.
